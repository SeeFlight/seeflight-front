WHAT IS SEEFLIGHT ?

Seeflight is an ecommerce travel platform that offers a fresh time saving method of organizing and displaying flight options to users searching to find the best prices
City to city search results yield multiple flight options for dates, showing the range of best roundtrip flight prices that have a departure over a 15 day period. The search result filters in the same window time of destination, return and stay besides price.

what cities are included ?

Multiple alternate cities are included in the search results, allowing to consider to any city at the best price and dates convenience. The platform will be available North and South American and European markets. We will make it easier to find and to choose where and when to travel

WHEN WILL it BE AVAILABLE ?

A website and mobile application for iOS and Android are being developed to show the best prices at Economy and Premium Economy fares. Our date to launch the desktop application is November 2.
